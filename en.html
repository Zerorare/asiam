Banquet terms

The essentials in brief — full terms are set out in the contract.

💰 Deposit and booking

  • Banquet up to 50 guests — ₩800,000 deposit
  • 50 to 100 guests — ₩1,000,000
  • Over 100 guests — ₩1,500,000
  • The deposit is non-refundable if the event is cancelled
  • Payment screenshot — to the organizer via WhatsApp

📋 Menu and timing

  • 1.5–2 months before the date — menu approval and exact guest count
  • The number of guests is fixed in the contract
  • The guest count cannot be reduced after signing
  • Coordination — with one responsible person named in the contract

🍽 Banquet at our restaurant

You get 5 hours of banquet time from the start time stated in the contract, plus an extra 1 hour for comfortable gathering and seating of late guests.

Example:

  • Guests invited — 5:00 pm
  • From 5:00 pm guests are seated and cold appetizers are served
  • Hot dishes are brought out in stages, following the event program
  • Official start of the banquet — 6:00 pm
  • Banquet time — 6:00 pm to 11:00 pm
Extension: +1 hour — ₩600,000
+2 hours — ₩1,100,000

🚚 Catering

  • Staff arrival time and preparation time are agreed with the customer in advance
  • They depend on the event format, number of guests and venue specifics

💳 Payment and fees

  • Service charge — +10%
  • Card payment — +10% VAT
  • Tax documents — +10%

👶 Children

  • Children 6–10 years old — ₩35,000 / person
  • Over 10 years old — adult rate
  • More than 10 children — a separate kids' menu is agreed individually

This information is for reference. Please confirm the exact terms with the organizer when signing the contract.
